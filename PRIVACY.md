# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 1st, 2026**

## Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any user data.

## What the Extension Does

This extension performs local text replacements on web pages you visit:

- "PlayStation" → "PayStation"
- "Play Has No Limits" → "Pay Has No Limits"

All replacements happen locally in your browser. No information about your browsing activity, the pages you visit, or the text on those pages is collected or sent anywhere.

## Permissions

The extension requires broad host permissions (`*://*/*`) to perform text replacements on any website you visit. This permission is used solely for modifying page content locally.

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

## Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be updated in this document.
